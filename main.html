Summary

Inception is a sci-fi thriller directed by Christopher Nolan, centered around a skilled thief, Dom Cobb, who steals information by infiltrating dreams. He's offered a chance to have his criminal history erased in exchange for implanting an idea into a target's subconscious — a process known as inception.
